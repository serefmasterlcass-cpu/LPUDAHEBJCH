
MEET THE PEOPLE BEHIND THE PRODUCTION
--------------------------------------
Jeff Batusbatusan, Director

Jeff Batusbatusan is a HUMSS Inclusivity student who serves as the Director of Ang Huling El Bimbo and the HUMSS Councilor of the Junior College Local Council. Since 2025, he has been quietly and steadily working behind the scenes to bring this theater production to life for Junior College, pouring his time and effort into every detail so that others could shine. Known for his charm, warmth and his genuine care for the people around him, Jeff leads not by standing above his team but by standing with them. This production is a shared achievement, and Jeff would be the first to say so.

Princess Masesar, Creative Director

Princess Masesar is a HUMSS Inclusivity student who serves as the Creative Director of the production and the Chairwoman of the HUMSS Association, leading her strand with both authority and heart. Recognized for her remarkable voice and her excellence in oral communication, Princess is a student whose talent speaks for itself in every room she enters. Hardworking and deeply passionate about the arts, she has poured her creativity and leadership into shaping the artistic soul of this production. She is, in every sense of the word, a force.
Seven Obeso
Seven Obeso is a HUMSS Inclusivity student who serves as the Musical Director of Ang Huling El Bimbo and President of Vocal Harmony. Pulling double duty as both the creative authority behind the music and a lead performer in the role of Older Joy, Seven brings a rare depth and quiet confidence to everything she does. With an extensive background in music and theater, she does not just perform a role, she inhabits it, making every moment feel earned and every note feel intentional. Her presence on this production, both on stage and behind it, is one of its greatest strengths.

Adrian De Paz, Stage and Backstage Director

Adrian De Paz is a HUMSS Inclusivity student who serves as the Stage and Backstage Director of the production. An intelligent and articulate student, Adrian is widely known for his skills as a competitive debater and his active membership in the Lycean House of Representatives. His sharp mind and ability to think on his feet translate seamlessly into the organized chaos of backstage management, where precision and calm under pressure are everything. 

Cassandra Willett, Public Relations and Marketing

Cassandra Willett is a HUMSS Inclusivity student who serves as the Public Relations Officer of the Junior College Local Council, as well as the Operations Head and a photojournalist for the school publication, PRIMUS, while also contributing to creatives and marketing teams across several organizations. With a strong creative instinct, she brings ideas to life and makes effective contributions wherever she’s involved.



Andrei Molina, Scriptwriter

Andrei Molina is a HUMSS Inclusivity student who serves as the Scriptwriter of the production. Intelligent, diligent, and genuinely outgoing, Andrei is a member of both the HUMSS Association, where he serves as Treasurer, and the school debate club. His command of language and his ability to craft compelling narratives make him a true pillar of this production. The words that the audience will hear on stage carry his thoughtfulness, his research, and his deep respect for the source material.

Teatephi Macaraeg, Audience Relations Manager

Teatephi Macaraeg is a HUMSS Inclusivity student who serves as the Audience Relations Manager and a key member of the marketing team for the production. Known for her artistic skills and her ability to connect with people genuinely, Teatephi is the bridge between the production and its audience. Whether it is coordinating audience experience or contributing creative ideas to the marketing effort, she approaches every task with talent and care. For any inquiries about the production, Teatephi is your first point of contact.



Jesswill Bermejo, Head Costume Designer and Handler

Jesswill Bermejo is a HUMSS Inclusivity student who serves as the Head Costume Designer and Handler of the production. A stunning and intelligent individual, Jesswill is recognized as a model under the ALBA, the Association of Lycean Brand Ambassadors, and serves as Vice President of the school fashion club Lynel Vestida. His keen eye for aesthetics and deep understanding of fashion have made him the perfect creative mind to dress the world of Ang Huling El Bimbo, ensuring every costume tells its own story on stage.

Princess Vequizo, Props Manager

Princess Vequizo is a HUMSS Inclusivity student who serves as the Props Manager of the production. Joyful, reliable, and quietly impactful, Princess may not always be in the spotlight of school events, but she is one of the most dependable people in any room she is in. Known across the HUMSS strand as a trustworthy friend and steadfast classmate, she also serves as Class Vice Mayor. Her steady hands and warm presence keep the production grounded, and behind every perfectly placed prop is her careful and thoughtful work.


Rhian Virtudazo, Head Choreographer

Rhian Virtudazo is a HUMSS Inclusivity student who serves as one of the Head Choreographers of the production. Intelligent, charming, and genuinely talented, Rhian is a student who brings a rare combination of discipline and artistry to everything she does. She serves as Secretary of the HUMSS Association and is a significant contributor to the school publication, making her presence felt far beyond the stage. Her choreography is a reflection of her character: precise, expressive, and full of life.

Princess Mallorca, Head Choreographer

Princess Mallorca is a HUMSS Inclusivity student who serves as one of the Head Choreographers of the production. Known for her dynamic dance performances, her competitive spirit as a badminton athlete, and her warm and infectious personality, Princess brings a unique energy to the production team. She moves through every challenge with joy and grace, both on and off the stage. Her choreography is a testament to her love for movement and her commitment to making every performance unforgettable.

Gio Bardos
Gio Bardos is a STEM-B Efficiency student who serves as one of the Head Choreographers of Ang Huling El Bimbo. What makes Gio remarkable is not just one thing but all of them together. He sings, he dances, and he choreographs with equal skill and ease, making him one of the true creative engines behind this production. Versatile, talented, and genuinely hard to keep up with, Gio brings an infectious energy to the team and a standard of excellence to every routine he builds. 


Dr. Are Jay Castro, Executive Producer and Dean of Junior College

Are Jay Castro serves as the Dean of the Junior College and the executive producer of Ang Huling El Bimbo. With an extensive background in events management and education, Dean Castro has been the guiding force and final authority behind the production. His approval, mentorship, and vision helped transform this ambitious project into reality. The AHEB Musical is yet another milestone in a long line of meaningful experiences he has brought to life for the students of LPU Davao, and a reflection of his unwavering commitment to holistic education.


Jeremy Zulueta, Producer

Jeremy Zulueta is the hands-on producer of Ang Huling El Bimbo and a cornerstone of its creation. Formerly a teacher in the Junior College, he now serves as the CSMC Supervisor, bringing with him years of experience in education and student development. His guidance, dedication, and passion for the craft have been instrumental in shaping the production from the ground up. A well-respected educator, Sir Zulueta has poured tremendous effort into nurturing the students behind this play, and his influence is felt in every aspect of the show.
