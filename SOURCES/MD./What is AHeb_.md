*AHEB* is a musical theatre play adapted and performed by the Grade 12 HUMSS students of LPU Davao. The production showcases the students’ creativity, talent, and collaborative effort in bringing a story to life through music, acting, and dance. The play explores themes of love, resilience, and self-discovery, reflecting the experiences and emotions of young individuals navigating life’s challenges. Through engaging musical numbers and dynamic performances, *AHEB* highlights not only the artistic abilities of the students but also the importance of storytelling as a powerful medium for expression and connection.
