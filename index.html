About the Show

A Story of Love,
Resilience & Discovery

Ang Huling El Bimbo is a musical theatre play adapted and performed by the Grade 12 HUMSS students of LPU Davao. Through engaging musical numbers and dynamic performances, the production explores the experiences and emotions of young individuals navigating life's challenges — themes of love, resilience, and self-discovery that resonate across generations.

Presented by the Lyceum of the Philippines University Davao Junior College, this production marks a landmark achievement of student artistry and collaboration.

Viewer Advisory: Contains mature themes, strobe lights, and loud sounds. Not suitable for very young or sensitive audiences.
