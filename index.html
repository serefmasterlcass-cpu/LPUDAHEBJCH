About the Show

A Story of Love,
Resilience & Discovery

Ang Huling El Bimbo is a musical theatre production brought to life by the HUMSS Junior College students of Lyceum of the Philippines University Davao. Featuring compelling musical numbers, expressive choreography, and emotionally driven performances, the show captures the journey of young individuals navigating friendship, love, loss, and personal growth. Its timeless themes of resilience and self-discovery offer a meaningful and relatable experience for audiences across generations.

Presented by the Lyceum of the Philippines University Davao Junior College, this production represents a defining milestone in student-led artistry. It reflects a high level of creative direction, discipline, and collaboration, highlighting the ability of young performers to deliver a polished and impactful theatrical experience.

Viewer Advisory: Contains mature themes, strobe lighting effects, and loud sounds. Viewer discretion is advised for very young or sensitive audiences.
